Expert Reviews

As an experience design professional, we often either work with existing products and services or review them as part of a competitive audit or benchmark. This helps identify areas for improvement in current or future products. These types of reviews are also crucial for communicating to product stakeholders what adjacent or competive solutions are doing better. Experience design research intersects with marketing and business intellignece.

Biz VoLTE Phone Review

Prior to tasks-based user studies, a UI/UX review was performed for components of the Biz VoLTE service on April 27th at Sach’s Insights in New York where a working prototype was setup. The following items were reviewed: Two Yealink desk phone models – T46G & T49G Companion mobile application Although not an exhaustive review of the BIZ VoLTE service was performed, findings from the brief assessment provided the opportunity to document areas for improvement of the user experience; particularly, with the desk phones. Key areas for improvement include: Improve design consistency of iconography, labeling and focus treatment across all service touch points. Employ a conversational tone and Verizon Brand voice for more friendly UI text.
