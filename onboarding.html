Employee Onboarding Experience Inquiry & Evolution Strategy Design

Project Summary

Role
  • Design Research Director
  • BX/EX/UX Sr. Design Research Lead
  • Sr. Lead Strategic Experience Designer

Ambition & Scope

The User Experience team within EY's global End User Technology Experience organization, brought new staff onboard from different countries and regions. A unique opportunity was available to assess the new hire onboarding experience from the perspective of five independent experts that had recently joined the firm - looking at all new staff touchpoints. The findings would be shared with EY's global HR/Talent organization to inform future experience enhancement strategy.



Result

Findings were used by the Talent organization to kick-off several large enhancement projects.

Grounded with with initial research, I began a strategic investigation on evolving the employee experience in a transformational way. My strategy was socialized with EY's Global Enterprise Solution Architects for feasibility analysis. Immediate value of the experience vision was recognized after technical review.

Approach

To achieve success, a mixed-method design research approach was followed, capturing qualitative data that was quantified. Workflow included investigate, document, analyze, and verify.

The Work

Employee Participant Journey Maps Produced


A general journey map framework was utilized by each reviewer to meticulously assess and document each step of their personal new hire experience. Positive and negative aspects of their experience were listed below key event milestones that each participant individually identified. Supporting artifacts were included where available and sensitive personal information was obscured. No additional requirements were placed upon a reviewer.
