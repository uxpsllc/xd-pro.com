Ambition & Scope

EY’s Global Risk Management team engaged Enterprise Technology to assist in the definition and delivery of a transformational Incident Management Experience (IMX). The aim of the project was to bring together a diverse ecosystem of information systems to create a streamlined, automated, and cohesive experience for executing command, control, and communications for situations that impact staff and business operations.

Technically, the solution would leverage Microsoft's cloud service platform to facilitate automation, efficiency, collaboration, and overall management of events - interfacing with the Everbridge Mass notification platform and other system's related data.

The solution would reach five global regions, 150 countries, 700 office locations, each with their own location-specific crisis management plan and readiness checklists for over 90 incident type categories - each with associated templates.


Result

My research led, abstract-to-concrete, user centered design approach, allowed for the successful creation of an MVP.

The tool was rolled out at EY’s Global Security conference in Boston, MA where the MVP was very well received by the international attendee audience. The Risk Management organizations leadership was eager to socialize and grow the experience. Additional funding was appropriated for version two enhancements.

Approach

To achieve success, a user-centric design approach was followed - research, analyze, define, design, & verify.

The Work

The project kicked off with an initial gathering of cross-functional business stakeholders from the Risk, Technology, and Experience Design organizations. A vision for the project was shared, covering the aspiration - what, why, who, and what success would look like.
