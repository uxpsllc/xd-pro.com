The Verizon Experience Design Strategy

Personal Role: Director User Experience Design & Strategist

It's not everyday one is afforded the opportunity to put into motion a plan that will help shape an industry and provide guidance on the direction of a $100B+, rapidly growing, telecom juggernaut. As an early industry UX'r and champion in 2006, I was fortunate to have the chance. Because of the CX/UX significance and corporate-cultural impact it made, I share this work.

Task: Create a Best-in-Class Verizon Experience

This charge, directly from the Corporate Chief Marketing Officer, would become the cornerstone for the direction Verizon and Verizon Wireless would head as a unified company.

My Approach: Insights Research > Eco-system Inventory > Vision Execution BluePrint

To achieve success would first require defining the scope of the charge, the problem being solved, and the direction to proceed. I formulated questions around the ultimate goal for success. Identified problems, challanges, and opportunities based on insights. Took inventory of everything including trends in culture, technology, and the product and service offerings of the corportation - even benchmarking the sector competition along with adjacent potential disruptors. My inquiry went as deep as to include looking at other strong consumer brands and what made them desirable to their market segment.
