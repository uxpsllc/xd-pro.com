Design & Innovation


Design isn’t just wireframes and UI comps — it spans product vision and the technical bedrock to scale and innovate. I flex fidelity—from quick sketches and AI-informed experiments that pressure-test ideas to production-ready systems that institutionalize them.

Service Design Wireframing



I use low-fidelity wireflows to communicate concepts and capture evolving requirements—keeping them live through development to quickly explore alternatives as user and stakeholder feedback shapes the solution.

Service Blueprinting

I use service blueprints to map the end-to-end journey—operations, touchpoints, and interactions—revealing pain points and aligning teams on improvements so the user experience and the business behind it work as one.

High Fidelity UX Mockups & Flows





I design UX flows that pair user journeys with system logic and conditional paths—shared as static maps or interactive Figma prototypes tied to a component-based design system for rapid testing—so I can switch fidelity on demand, from low-fi to production-polished.

Design Prototyping



I keep feedback continuous and prototypes right-sized—from quick paper sketches to realistic system simulations—to test ideas, validate requirements, and de-risk decisions. I use the fastest tool for the job (AI tools, Figma, Miro/Mural, and HTML) to capture evidence and keep delivery moving.

Design Specifications & Requirements






I publish code-ready specs: pixel-perfect responsive layouts with grids, spacing, and type ramps encoded as design tokens; component blueprints with props/variants, state matrices, and accessibility semantics; millisecond-level motion (durations, delays, easing, reduced-motion). Each package includes redlines, interactive prototypes, localization/RTL guidance, performance budgets, and testable UX acceptance criteria to ensure design–engineering parity.

Contextual Inquiry


I conduct in-context observation — watching real workflows that uncover artifacts and decision points — surfacing needs and constraints that surveys miss. The evidence translates into clear requirements that de-risk delivery, spark new solution ideas, and measurably reduce support costs while transforming legacy processes.

Workshops & Collaborative Design


I facilitate design-thinking workshops—virtual, asynchronous, or in-person—to align stakeholders and move fast. For global teams, I plan and moderate sessions in Miro, Mural, or FigJam, and adapt the format for broader focus groups when needed.
